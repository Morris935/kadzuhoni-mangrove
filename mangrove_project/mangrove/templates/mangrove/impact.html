Our Impact

Discover the positive changes we've made for the environment and our community

Mangrove Reforestation and Nurseries

We have successfully planted over 7,000 mangrove trees, contributing to the preservation of our coastal ecosystem. Our nurseries contain over 45,000 seedlings, ensuring the sustainability of our mangrove restoration efforts.

Formation of Community Groups

We have formed five community groups focused on sustainable development and environmental conservation:

Kadzuhoni Fishing Group

Focused on sustainable fishing practices that protect marine biodiversity while supporting local fishermen.

Kadzuhoni Beekeeping Group

Engages in mangrove-based beekeeping, producing high-quality honey and promoting conservation through economic activities.

Kadzuhoni Ecotourism Group

Promotes eco-friendly tourism, offering educational tours that highlight the importance of mangrove conservation.

Kadzuhoni Sports and Culture Group

Fosters community bonding through sports and cultural activities, encouraging youth engagement in conservation efforts.

Salt Making Group

Focuses on sustainable salt-making practices, utilizing natural resources from the mangrove ecosystem responsibly.

Strengthening Community and Environment

By creating these groups, we provide sustainable income-generating opportunities, improve community well-being, and foster a deep connection between our people and the environment.

Environmental Education and Advocacy

Through workshops, community meetings, and outreach programs, we raise awareness about the importance of mangroves, climate change, and conservation efforts. Our mission is to continue expanding our impact, creating a sustainable future for both the community and the environment.

Looking Ahead

We are committed to planting more trees, growing our nurseries, and involving even more community members in our conservation efforts. Together, we are shaping a better future for our community and the environment that sustains us.
