About Kadzuhoni Mangrove Conservation

Preserving our mangrove forests for future generations

Our Mission

our mission is to protect and restore mangrove ecosystems in the coastal regions of Kenya. We believe in the power of community-driven conservation efforts and dedication to raising awareness about the importance of mangroves will maintain biodiversity, mitigate climate change, and support local livelihoods.

Who We Are

Kadzuhoni Mangrove Conservation is a registered Community-Based Organization (EST - 2023) committed to safeguarding mangrove ecosystems. Our team is composed of passionate environmentalists, local leaders, scientists, and volunteers, all working together to ensure that mangrove forests continue to benefit both nature and people - Nature Matters to all of us.

What We Do

We engage in various activities such as mangrove reforestation, community education, and sustainable livelihood projects to ensure the long-term health of our coastal ecosystems.

Why Mangroves Matter

Mangroves play a crucial role in protecting coastlines from erosion, providing habitats for marine life, and acting as carbon sinks. Their conservation is vital for ecological balance and community well-being.

Get Involved

Join us in our efforts to conserve mangroves! Whether through volunteering, donations, or spreading the word, your support is valuable in making a positive impact.
