What We Do

At Kadzuhoni Mangrove Conservation, we focus on the conservation of mangroves and other aquatic species through community-led initiatives. Our key activities include:

  • Mangrove Reforestation: Planting mangrove trees to restore ecosystems.
  • Mangrove Nurseries: Cultivating seedlings to support reforestation.
  • Community Empowerment: Supporting sustainable activities like fishing, beekeeping, ecotourism, and more.

Our Vision

We envision a future where the mangrove ecosystem is fully restored, benefiting both the environment and the communities that depend on it. Our approach ensures long-term positive outcomes for people and nature.
