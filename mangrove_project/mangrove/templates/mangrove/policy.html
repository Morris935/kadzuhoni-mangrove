Advocacy & Policy Development

Shaping Policies for Sustainable Mangrove Conservation

Our Advocacy Efforts

Kadzuhoni Mangrove Conservation is dedicated to advocating for policies that protect and restore mangrove ecosystems. We work closely with Magarini CFA, government bodies like Kenya Forest Services (KFS), non-governmental organizations, and communities to shape policies that promote sustainable development and environmental stewardship.

Our advocacy efforts aim to raise awareness about the importance of mangroves in coastal resilience, climate change mitigation, and biodiversity conservation.

Policy Development Initiatives

Mangrove Protection Laws

We work with local governments to develop and strengthen laws that ensure the long-term protection of mangrove forests.

Climate Change Policies

Our research and advocacy contribute to climate change policies that emphasize the role of mangroves in carbon sequestration and coastal defense.

Community Land Rights

We advocate for community-based land rights to ensure that local communities benefit from sustainable mangrove management.

Current Advocacy Projects

Our current advocacy projects focus on creating a supportive policy environment that encourages mangrove conservation and restoration. We engage with policymakers at all levels to ensure that mangrove ecosystems are recognized as vital components of climate resilience and sustainable development.

We also collaborate with community leaders and organizations to ensure that policies reflect the needs and values of the people who rely on mangrove resources for their livelihoods.

Advocacy Highlights

Our key advocacy achievements include:

Creation of New Protected Areas

We successfully lobbied for the designation of new mangrove-protected areas along Kenya’s coast.

Community Involvement in Decision-Making

We advocated for greater community participation in conservation decision-making processes, empowering local voices.

Contributions to Climate Policies

Our organization contributed to national climate policies that highlight the role of mangroves in carbon capture and coastal protection.
